Fashion Design：

1. Chinese Emperor Living in the West

  • Inspiration

    • I was inspired by the last Chinese emperor-Pu Yi. For centuries, Chinese emperors wore imperial dragon robes.But Pu Yi received western influences and enjoyed wearing suites, dark sunglasses, and even got an English name- Henry. He was an important person who influenced the chinese constume history.

  • Design concept

    • Black suits and yellow imperial robes are the main colors. The combination of chinese and western costumes also represents rigidness and softness, which makes the design motif- chinese totem and western cuttings.

Marketing Project：

Integrated marketing communications- Lotus

The subject of this marketing communications study is the Lotus Eletre line. There are six parts to this study. The first is an analysis of the target audience and the condition of the brand, including information relating to the reasons for the selection of the target and the challenges the brand faces.

The report then discusses marketing objectives and strategies in order to comprehend the existing plans of the brand, improve the marketing strategy and propose new solutions to ensure that the brand is able to compete with other brands.

Finally, a budget and a review of the marketing strategy are included to assist the company in the planning of its marketing budget and to determine whether these techniques are beneficial for facilitating further brand improvement.
