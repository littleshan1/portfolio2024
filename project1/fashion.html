Chinese Emperor Living in the West：

Inspiration:

I was inspired by the last Chinese emperor-Pu Yi. For centuries, Chinese emperors wore imperial dragon robes.But Pu Yi received western influences and enjoyed wearing suites, dark sunglasses, and even got an English name- Henry. He was an important person who influenced the chinese constume history.

Design concept:

Black suits and yellow imperial robes are the main colors. The combination of chinese and western costumes also represents rigidness and softness, which makes the design motif- chinese totem and western cuttings.

Be Myself：

Inspiration:

Focusing on myself to present my painting style. I like wearing loose and colorful clothes. These feathers are further accentuated.

Design concept:

Each print has its own meaning. In the crowd, we all crave to be the unique one. Physically I have my own symbol. We need to make reverse thinking so that we will not become the fish going downstream.
