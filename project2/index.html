Japan Map

Japan is geographically located in East Asia, bordered to the east by the Pacific Ocean, and to the west by the Sea of Japan, the Korea Strait, the East China Sea, and the Eurasian continent, including Siberia, the Korean Peninsula, and China. To the south, Japan is adjacent to the Philippines Sea, Taiwan, and the Mariana Islands, and to the north, it is bordered by the Sōya Strait, the Sea of Okhotsk, the Kuril Islands, and Sakhalin. Japan enjoys a pleasant climate throughout the year, with distinct changes between the four seasons.

The southern regions are warm, while the northern regions become colder. In particular, during winter, the northwest monsoon brings heavy snow to the Japan Sea side. On the other hand, the Pacific side remains dry and clear, often referred to as "winter sunshine.
