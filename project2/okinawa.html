- Okinawa ｜ 沖縄 （おきなわ）-

- Okinawa -

American Village｜ 美國村（美浜アメリカンビレッジ）

American Village is a popular entertainment and shopping complex located in Chatan, Okinawa. It features an American-themed atmosphere with a mix of Western-style restaurants, boutiques, and arcades. The area is known for its vibrant nightlife, scenic views of the ocean, and attractions like the large Ferris wheel, making it a lively spot for both locals and tourists to enjoy dining, shopping, and entertainment.

Kokusai Dori ｜ 国際通（こくさいとおり）

Kokusai Dori is a lively street in Naha, Okinawa, known for its vibrant atmosphere and wide range of shops, restaurants, and local markets. This bustling street is a great place to experience Okinawan culture, offering everything from traditional souvenirs to delicious street food. It’s also home to various entertainment spots, making it a must-visit destination for both tourists and locals.

Tokashiku ｜ 渡嘉敷島（とかしきじま）

Tokashiku, located on Tokashiki Island in Okinawa, is known for its clear waters and beautiful beaches, perfect for snorkeling and diving. Tokashiku Beach offers a peaceful escape, while the island’s coral reefs and hiking trails provide great outdoor activities.
