Universal Studios Japan ｜（ユニバーサル・スタジオ・ジャパン）

Universal Studios Japan (USJ) in Osaka is a top theme park featuring immersive attractions based on popular films. Highlights include The Wizarding World of Harry Potter with its Hogwarts Castle, and Super Nintendo World, where guests can step into the world of Mario. Other themed areas like Jurassic Park and Minion Park add to the excitement, making USJ a must-visit for movie fans and families alike.

Osaka Castle ｜ 大坂城（おおさかじょう）

Osaka Castle is a historic landmark in Japan, known for its beautiful architecture and surrounding park. Originally built in the 16th century, it played a major role in Japan’s unification. The castle is especially popular during cherry blossom season, when its gardens come alive with blooming sakura, offering visitors a scenic and cultural experience.

Dotonbori ｜ 道顿堀（どうとんぼり）

Dotonbori is Osaka’s lively entertainment and dining district, famous for its neon lights, bustling atmosphere, and iconic Glico running man sign. Known for street food like takoyaki and okonomiyaki, Dotonbori is a must-visit for its vibrant nightlife and unique culinary delights.
